About

This data analysis initiative aims to comprehensively examine global data pertaining to mental health issues. By aggregating and analyzing data from diverse sources worldwide, our goal is to gain a deeper understanding of the prevalence, trends, and associated factors related to mental health challenges on a global scale. This research seeks to inform evidence-based interventions and policies to better address the critical issue of mental health and promote well-being across diverse populations worldwide.

STORY

In Tableau, a story is a feature that allows users to create a cohesive and compelling narrative using data visualizations. A story is essentially a sequence of sheets and dashboards that are combined to tell a data-driven story or present a specific insight or analysis. Tableau's story feature enables users to arrange and annotate visualizations in a logical order, providing context and guiding the audience through the data's key points.

DASHBOARD

Dashboards in Tableau are powerful tools that allow users to visually explore and analyze data in a highly interactive and intuitive way. They serve as a central hub where various visualizations, charts, and graphs can be combined to provide a comprehensive overview of data.
With the ability to connect to various data sources and the flexibility to customize the layout and design, Tableau dashboards enable data-driven decision-making and storytelling by transforming raw data into meaningful and actionable information.

Learnings

Our group project in mental health data analytics taught us crucial skills in data cleaning, visualization, and dashboard creation. We honed our ability to handle real-world data challenges, ensuring data quality and consistency. Crafting meaningful visualizations improved our communication skills, and building a dashboard emphasized the importance of data storytelling. We also gained a deeper understanding of ethical considerations in data analysis within the context of mental health.
